About Us

Founded in 2018, Greenvalley EcoTech Waste Solutions was established to address the urgent need for sustainable waste management in Greenvalley, Acornhoek South Africa. What started as a small community-driven project has grown into a trusted provider of eco-friendly solutions for households, businesses and municipalities.

Our Mission

To promote sustainable waste management through innovative solutions that reduce environmental impact and empower communities to live greener, cleaner lives.

Our Vision

To become a leading provider of eco-friendly waste solutions in Southern Africa, driving positive environmental change and setting the standard for sustainable living

Our Values

  • Sustainability - Protecting the environment for future generations.
  • Community - Empowering individuals and organisations to make a difference.
  • Innovation - Creating smart and practical waste management solutions.
  • Integrity - Delivering honest, transparent and reliable services.
